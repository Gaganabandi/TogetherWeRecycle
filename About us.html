About Us

At Together We Recycle, we believe that small actions can lead to big changes.
Our mission is to reduce plastic pollution by making it easy for people to dispose of their plastic waste responsibly.
Through our platform, registered members can schedule plastic pickups or drop-offs, and we ensure that all collected waste is properly recycled.
We aim to build a community that cares for the environment and takes action.
By working together, we’re not just managing waste—we’re creating a cleaner, greener future. Let’s recycle. Let’s do it togetther.
